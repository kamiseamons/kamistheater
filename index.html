Meet Your Instructor

Your kids are in great hands

Kami Seamons

Director, Choreographer & Vocal Instructor

I performed with the original Anastasia cast at Lincoln Center in New York City, understudied Persephone in Hadestown, and have been on stage my whole life. I want to help your kids fall in love with theater the same way I did. This camp is about building confidence, having fun, and putting on an amazing show together. I'm also saving for college, so every registration means the world to me!

Credits: Anastasia (Lincoln Center NYC), Hadestown (Persephone u/s), Tarzan (Terk), Acting Up at SCERA Theatre, American Heritage Chamber Choir & Improv Troupe
