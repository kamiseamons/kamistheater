The Show

Every week ends with a live performance

Once Upon a Stage presents

Magic Through the Ages

A 20-minute Disney-inspired revue featuring beloved favorites from classic to modern. Every camper performs on stage with singing, choreography, and narration, and every kid gets their own featured moment. There's nothing like putting on a show together to build friendships that last a lifetime.

Family and friends are invited to the Saturday show at 3:00 PM to see what their camper has been working on all week!
